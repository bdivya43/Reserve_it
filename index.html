About the app

An app that simplifies dining and event planning by offering restaurant table reservations, banquet hall bookings for events, and catering services. Users can easily book tables, secure venues for celebrations, and arrange customized catering with menu selection and delivery, all in one platform for seamless convenience.

Restaurant table

Booking a table in a restaurant through our app which allows users to secure a dining spot in advance. It eliminates the need for phone calls or waiting in line by providing real-time availability of tables

Banquet hall

Booking a banquet hall in a restaurant through our app that has some features like menu, ambiance, and online messaging option provides a basic yet functional approach to event planning.

Catering service

Booking catering in a restaurant through our app that offers the menu and online messaging features provides a straightforward and simple experience.
